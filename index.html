Poems of common threads
running through life
showing why falling in love
is the most intelligent thing you can do.

These poems sing of the natural beauty of the link between intelligence and morality. Humans form a kind of thinking entity new in the universe. We are at our most powerful, successful and magnificent when each and every individual is allowed, enabled and inspired to make more decisions in their lives. Freedom works, intelligence is beautiful. Release from illusions that keep people apart is not only empowering for all, it opens doors to those you love, to letting sunshine and magic into life.
